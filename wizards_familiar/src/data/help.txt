To move type: 
*forward or *f
*back or *b
*left or *l
*right or *r
*up or *u
*down or *d

If a word is *highlighted, type its name to interact with it. 

To view your inventory type:
*inventory or *i

To read the description of a room again type:
*description or *desc

To view this list of commands again type:
*help or *h

There is a map in the top right corner of the screen!
